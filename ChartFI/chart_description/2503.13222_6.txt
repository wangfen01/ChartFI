The two correlation heatmaps illustrate how similarly different large language models perform across a shared evaluation suite and how various task categories correlate in their evaluated performance. In the model-to-model view, most pairs cluster in high positive correlation, indicating that models tend to rise and fall together across the benchmark and that the suite largely rewards a common underlying capability profile. Specifically, LLMs from each model family show more or less internal correlation with each other, especially in the GPT, Mistral, and Llama families, while models showing inheritance relationships demonstrate similar capability profiles, with GPT, Claude, and Llama families each exhibiting characteristic performance signatures. Reasoning-based models such as DeepSeek models and GPT-o-series also show similarity with each other, whereas the Mixtral-8x7B-Instruct row and column show markedly weaker alignment with many other models, suggesting a distinct strength–weakness signature relative to the broader group. In the task-to-task view, the capabilities of Dynamic Environment, Dynamic Instruction, Multitasking, and Recursive Reasoning do positively correlate with each other, forming a tight cluster consistent with a shared dependence on general adaptability and instruction-following. Triggering and memorization also correlate with each other, showing mid-range relationships with the adaptability cluster. Conversely, tasks falling within the “contradiction resolution” category, namely Privacy Protection, Personalization, and Prioritization, are less correlated with the other tasks. Privacy Protection is weakly or even negatively correlated with several categories, signaling a tradeoff where stronger performance on personalization-style behaviors may coincide with weaker privacy-preserving behavior, while Prioritization is the most different from all other tasks. Overall, the dominant pattern is a strong general-performance factor across both models and tasks, with contradiction resolution tasks standing out as the clearest axis of divergence and potential tension with other desirable capabilities.

This series of boxplots illustrates the distribution of GNN4IP similarity scores for different LLMs in LLMPirate’s framework, summarizing these values across all 32 netlists for each LLM. The visualization arranges the models vertically to facilitate comparison against a detection threshold, marked by a blue dashed line at zero. A primary observation from the data is that the C499-* netlists have the highest similarity scores across all LLMs, with models such as Gemini, Llama3 8B, CL 7B, and CL 13B consistently achieving values near the upper limit of 1. In contrast, the c432-* netlists located on the left side of the charts generally exhibit scores below the detection threshold, indicating consistently lower similarity across the board. The c880-* netlists demonstrate more model-specific variability; while CoPilot, Claude, CL-13B, GPT series and Gemini remain largely below the threshold for these inputs, Llama3 8B diverges from this pattern by maintaining positive similarity scores. Furthermore, the spread of the data indicates that while models like GPT4 show wider variance in their scores for the c880-* group, the CL series and Gemini display tighter distributions, particularly within the high-scoring C499-* segment.

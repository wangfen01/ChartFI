This heatmap visualizes agent accuracies on various dataset slices, organizing performance metrics across question subsets clustered by their effective difficulty, ranging from "V.Hard" Abduction tasks to "Easy" Mixed Semantics. The color-coded grid reveals distinct performance tiers, where Abduction (V.Hard) and Mixed Bag emerge as the most challenging categories, characterized by significantly lower scores across most machine learning models compared to human benchmarks. In these complex reasoning tasks, AI systems, including GPT-4-TURBO, struggle with these, highlighting a marked disparity with human accuracy; for instance, while the Human Team achieves 85.2% on Abduction, GPT-4 Turbo drops to 49.5%, and Base LLMs plummet to a mere 1.9%. Conversely, the visualization demonstrates that Instruction-tuned LLMs outperform base ones in moderately difficult science questions and general tasks, showing a clear progression in model capability. At the top tier of model performance, GPT-4 Omni exhibits exceptional results, consistently achieving high accuracy scores and surpassing single human players, particularly in categories like Mixed Abduction and GeoPol 2 where it reaches near-perfect scores.

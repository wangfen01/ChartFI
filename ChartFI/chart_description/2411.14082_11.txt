This chart compares the factorization time of the CKTSO solver against NICSLU and PARDISO across 56 benchmarks, with the vertical axis representing time in seconds on a logarithmic scale. The performance is evaluated under two configurations for each solver, specifically comparing single-threaded and 16-threaded execution. It can be observed that CKTSO generally has the smallest factorization time, maintaining a consistent performance advantage across the dataset. Specifically, CKTSO is the fastest for 50 out of the 56 benchmarks among the compared solvers, indicated by the blue lines consistently tracking below the orange and green series. Furthermore, the efficiency of the algorithm is underscored by the finding that sequential CKTSO is even faster than parallel NICSLU and PARDISO for half of the benchmarks, highlighting a significant reduction in computational cost relative to established alternatives.

The sunburst chart illustrates the question-answer distribution of WebUIBench across various evaluation dimensions and tasks, comprising a total of 21,793 question-answer pairs with an average of 10.68 pairs per webpage screenshot. Finally, the WebUI-to-Code segment focuses on Full Webpage and Webpage Slice generation, indicating a comprehensive approach to translating visual interfaces into functional code. Overall, the benchmark exhibits a sophisticated hierarchy that prioritizes element localization, text-based attribute recognition, and structural code manipulation, providing a balanced framework for evaluating multimodal web intelligence.

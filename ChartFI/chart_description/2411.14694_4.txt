This set of charts presents boxplots of revenues for different generators in the IEEE 30-bus system, segmented into four distinct operational scenarios covering lossless and lossy Optimal Power Flow (OPF) with block-form and quadratic-form bidding curves. The visualization is organized into six groups representing the GenCos owning one of the six strategic generators, respectively, with revenue performance plotted against specific levels of information labeled M, R, II, III, IV, and V. A critical evaluation of the revenue distributions demonstrates clear performance hierarchies; specifically, the proposed method outperforms the benchmark using empirical probability (V) in all levels of information. While the RDC-like method (R) performs slightly better than the benchmark using empirical probability (V), it generally falls short of the optimization achieved by the new approach, as the proposed method outperforms the RDC-like method in most of the cases. This trend holds consistently across the different bidding curve structures and transmission loss assumptions, highlighting that while the RDC-like approach offers a marginal improvement over the empirical baseline, the proposed method consistently secures higher median revenues for the strategic generators.

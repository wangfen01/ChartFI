This set of heatmap matrices displays the win rate matrix of human and ten MLLM models, evaluated by human, GPT-4o, and IntJudge, respectively, across FDT and w/o Tie settings. The heat map indicates win-loss relations, where warmer colors represent higher win rates and cooler colors vice versa, revealing a clear stratification in model performance. Notably, GPT-4o+ DALL·E-3 and Gemini1.5+ Flux achieve the strongest win rates, consistently appearing in the upper echelons of the matrices with predominantly red and orange cells. These top-performing models demonstrate exceptional capability, as their generations are even comparable to human-annotated output under GPT evaluation, where the visual distinction between the model outputs and human baselines becomes minimal. In contrast, the remaining models, such as MiniGPT-5 and GILL, display a prevalence of cooler tones when matched against these leading systems, underscoring a significant performance disparity within the current landscape of multimodal models.

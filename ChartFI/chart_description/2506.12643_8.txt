The stacked area chart illustrates the monthly sentiment of HN GH-AI comments from May 2022 through May 2024, revealing that overall engagement is driven primarily by neutral sentiment, which consistently accounts for the largest share of volume. Activity remains low and steady throughout 2022 before shifting sharply upward in late 2022 and early 2023, culminating in a pronounced peak in March 2023 where total comments exceed 600, largely due to a massive surge in neutral volume alongside a noticeable lift in positive and a smaller uptick in negative sentiment. Disregarding the neutral sentiment, we also see more positive comments in HN GH-AI stories than negative ones, an observation that applies throughout the whole analyzed period of two years. Following the March 2023 peak, total comment volume falls back but maintains a significantly higher baseline than in 2022, characterized by cyclical bursts roughly every few months through mid-2023 and into early 2024. During these elevated periods, such as in July 2023 and March 2024, neutral sentiment expands most significantly while positive sentiment rises in tandem and negative sentiment increases only modestly, suggesting that higher attention periods amplify all sentiments without materially altering the established hierarchy. The March 2023 event stands out as a unique one-off step change in volume, yet the underlying sentiment balance remains stable, with positive feedback consistently outweighing negative reactions across the entire two-year duration.

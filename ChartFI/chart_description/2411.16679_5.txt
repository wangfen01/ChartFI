This heatmap visualizes the evolution of a model's knowledge acquisition and reasoning capabilities over approximately 550,000 pretraining steps, specifically tracking the logical chain connecting Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation (e_1) to the Philippines (e_2) and finally to the anthem Lupang Hinirang (e_3). The chart displays binary states for data co-appearance and query accuracy, where blue represents a true condition and red represents false. Early in the training process, the data indicate that the model consistently sees (e_1, e_2) and (e_2, e_3) together across multiple pretraining steps, which serves as a precursor to functional knowledge. Following this exposure, the model starts to correctly predict the answer to the single-hop queries, with the second single-hop query showing consistent accuracy around the 250,000-step mark. A critical temporal relationship is observed where, after the model starts to learn to correctly answer the single-hop queries, the model starts to learn to correctly answer the multi-hop queries. This progression culminates in a significant finding: the bottom row remains consistently red, indicating that e_1 and e_3 never co-appeared in the training data. Despite this absence of direct connection, the successful resolution of the complex query demonstrates the emergence of latent multi-hop reasoning ability even when e_1 and e_3 have never co-appeared in any sequence throughout pretraining, highlighting the model's capacity to bridge independent facts into a coherent logical chain.

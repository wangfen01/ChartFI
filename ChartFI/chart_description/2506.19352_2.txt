This discrepancy reveals that previous metrics, such as ACC, fail to detect these “glitches”, leading to inflated scores by overlooking internal inconsistencies. Conversely, ACC_atom identifies these deviations, offering a more granular assessment of persona fidelity in long-form generations and exposing a systematic gap where strong overall ACC does not translate into comparable ACC_atom for lower-level personas. While High persona results remain tightly grouped near the top of the scale at the extreme high end of ACC, implying both higher peak performance and better reliability, the Low persona group continues to show pronounced variability and sharp shortfalls that only a granular metric can accurately capture.
